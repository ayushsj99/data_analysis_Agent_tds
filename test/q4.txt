 Tallest Buildings (Handling Units & Messy Data)
This tests the agent's ability to clean data with mixed units (meters/feet) and handle potentially missing or inconsistent entries.

Prompt:

Scrape the list of tallest buildings from Wikipedia. It is at the URL:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_tallest_buildings

Answer the following questions and respond with a JSON array of strings containing the answer.

How many buildings on the list are taller than 500 meters?

What is the average height (in meters) of the top 20 tallest buildings?

Which city has the most buildings in the top 50?

Draw a histogram showing the distribution of building heights (in meters) for all buildings on the list. Use 10 bins. Return it as a base-64 encoded data URI under 100,000 bytes.
